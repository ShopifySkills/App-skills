# Complete the Look — Copy Templates

Use these as starting points for bundle PDP and cart copy. Adapt to brand voice.

## Bundle headline (above the fold)

- "Complete your look — add the set"
- "Get everything you need for a lasting base"
- "One click: foundation + application + finish"
- "Complete the Look — [Bundle name]"

## Subhead / value line

- "Foundation + sponge + setting spray. Everything for a flawless base in one click."
- "Most customers complete the look — add the set and save [X% / $X]."
- "No guesswork: the exact combo our artists use."

## CTA (primary)

- "Add complete look to cart"
- "Add set to cart"
- "Get the set"
- "Complete my look"

## CTA (secondary — single item)

- "Add [product name] only"
- "Just this item"

## Bullet list (what’s in the bundle)

- "[Product 1] — [one benefit]"
- "[Product 2] — [one benefit]"
- "[Product 3] — [one benefit]"
- "One click. One complete look."

## Social proof / urgency (optional)

- "X% of customers add the complete look."
- "Best value when you complete the set."
- "Limited-time set price."

## Cart line (when bundle is in cart)

- "Part of Complete the Look — [Bundle name]"
- "Set: [Bundle name] — [link to edit or remove from set]"

## Compliance

- Avoid medical or efficacy claims (e.g. "cures", "treats").
- "Long-lasting", "flawless", "easy application" are positioning, not guarantees.
