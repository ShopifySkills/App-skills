# Complete Lip Look Bundle — WooCommerce Guidance

## 1. What to call it

**Recommended names (pick one or combine):**

- **Complete Lip Look** — Clear, benefit-led; implies “everything you need.”
- **Lip Look Bundle** — Short, easy to scan in menus and on product pages.
- **Lip Essentials Set** — Emphasises “must-haves” and value.
- **Lip Trio** — Simple and product-count based (liner + lipstick + gloss).

**Best default:** **“Complete Lip Look”** or **“Complete Lip Look Bundle”** — they describe the outcome (a full lip look) and work well in search, menus, and on the product page.

---

## 2. Where the bundle should appear

| Location | Why |
|----------|-----|
| **Single product pages (liner, lipstick, gloss)** | Shoppers who add one item are your main cross-sell audience. Show the bundle as a clear “Complete the look” or “Get the full set” block (above or below add-to-cart, or in a sticky/sidebar). |
| **Cart page** | After they add one lip product, offer the bundle as a one-click “Upgrade to Complete Lip Look” so they can replace or add the set. |
| **Category / shop archive** | List the bundle like a normal product so it appears when they browse “Lip” or “Makeup.” Use a distinct image (e.g. three products together) and a “Bundle” or “Set” badge. |
| **Checkout (optional)** | A small “Add Complete Lip Look” link or one-click add can work if your theme/plugin supports it, but cart is usually enough. |

**Priority:**  
1) Single product pages (liner / lipstick / gloss).  
2) Cart.  
3) Category/shop listing.

---

## 3. Best way to phrase the add-to-cart for the set

**Goals:** One-click add, clear value, no confusion that it’s a set of three.

**Button / CTA copy:**

- **Primary:** **“Add Complete Lip Look to cart”** or **“Add set to cart”**
- **Value-focused:** **“Add full set — Liner + Lipstick + Gloss”**
- **Action + benefit:** **“Get the full look — Add bundle”**

**Supporting line (under or near the button):**

- “One click adds liner, lipstick & gloss.”
- “Liner + lipstick + gloss in one click.”
- “Complete your look — all three in one add.”

**If you show price:**

- “Complete Lip Look — £XX” or “Full set — £XX (save £X).”

**Avoid:**  
Vague labels like “Add to cart” with no mention of “set” or “bundle,” or “Buy now” without clarifying it’s three products.

---

## Summary

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| **Name** | “Complete Lip Look” or “Complete Lip Look Bundle” |
| **Where** | (1) Single product pages for liner/lipstick/gloss, (2) Cart, (3) Lip/category listing |
| **Add-to-cart phrasing** | “Add Complete Lip Look to cart” with a subline: “Liner + lipstick + gloss in one click.” |

Implement the bundle as a WooCommerce product (grouped or composite product, or via a bundle plugin) so “Add to cart” adds the whole set in one click.
