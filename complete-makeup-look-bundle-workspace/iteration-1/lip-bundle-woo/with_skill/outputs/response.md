# Complete Lip Look Bundle — Strategy (WooCommerce)

## 1) Summary

- **Current gap**: Customers buy lip liner, lipstick, or lip gloss individually and return later for the rest. There is no one-click way to add the full lip look, which increases friction and leaves AOV and conversion on the table.
- **Recommended bundle type**: **Complete Lip Look** — lip liner + lipstick + lip gloss as a single, named bundle with one-click add-to-cart.
- **Top 3 actions**:
  1. Create a virtual/composite product or bundle in WooCommerce for "Complete Lip Look" (liner + lipstick + gloss).
  2. Surface the bundle on each lip-product PDP with a primary CTA and clear secondary "Add [item] only" option.
  3. Track AOV, bundle attach rate, and PDP→cart conversion to validate and iterate.
- **Metrics to watch**: AOV (all orders and bundle vs non-bundle), bundle attach rate (% of orders containing the bundle), conversion rate on PDPs where the bundle is shown; optionally refund/return rate for bundle vs single-item.

---

## 2) Bundle Definition

- **Logic**: Group by *look* (lip) — one coherent "complete lip" outcome, not random cross-sell.
- **Core bundle** (name + contents + one-line value):
  - **"Complete Lip Look"** — Lip Liner + Lipstick + Lip Gloss — *"Everything you need for a defined, polished lip in one click."*
- **Rules**: Three items per bundle (liner, lipstick, gloss); one hero product per PDP (e.g. lipstick) with the bundle built around it; always allow adding the single item only.
- **Naming**: Use **"Complete Lip Look"** consistently in PDP, cart, and analytics so reporting is straightforward.

---

## 3) Naming and Copy

- **Bundle name**: **Complete Lip Look** (short, benefit-led).
- **PDP/CTA**:
  - **Primary**: "Add complete lip look to cart" or "Add Complete Lip Look to cart".
  - **Secondary**: "Add [Lip Liner / Lipstick / Lip Gloss] only" (per product).
- **Copy blocks**:
  - **Headline**: "Complete your lip look — add the set."
  - **Subhead**: "Lip liner + lipstick + lip gloss. Everything for a defined, polished lip in one click."
  - **Bullet list**:
    - Lip Liner — defines and shapes.
    - Lipstick — colour and coverage.
    - Lip Gloss — shine and finish.
    - One click. One complete lip look.
  - **Social proof (optional)**: "Most customers add the complete lip look." or "X% of customers add the set."
- **Tone**: Match brand (clean/minimal or playful); avoid jargon; focus on outcome (defined lip, one-click convenience).

---

## 4) Placement and UX (WooCommerce-friendly)

- **PDP**: Show the bundle offer above the fold on every lip product page (liner, lipstick, gloss). Include:
  - Headline + subhead as above.
  - One primary button: **"Add complete lip look to cart"** (or "Add set to cart") that adds all three in one action.
  - Bundle price and per-item breakdown (e.g. "Complete Lip Look — £XX.XX (liner + lipstick + gloss)").
  - Secondary link/button per product: **"Add [Lip Liner / Lipstick / Lip Gloss] only"** so users can still buy a single item.
- **One-click add set**: Prefer a single "Add Complete Lip Look to cart" action (WooCommerce: composite product, product bundle plugin, or custom "add bundle to cart" that adds the three SKUs). Keep "Add [item] only" visible so the bundle is never the only path.
- **Cart**: When the bundle is added, show a line such as "Part of Complete Lip Look" and optional link to edit or remove from set, so the set is recognizable in the cart.
- **Collection / landing**: Optional "Complete the Look" or "Lip Sets" section linking to the Complete Lip Look bundle for discovery.

---

## 5) Metrics and Validation

- **Primary**:
  - **AOV**: All orders; compare AOV for orders that include the bundle vs orders that do not.
  - **Bundle attach rate**: % of sessions or orders that include the "Complete Lip Look" bundle.
  - **Conversion**: PDP → cart (or checkout) for pages where the bundle is shown; compare with periods or pages without the bundle.
- **Secondary**: Refund/return rate for bundle vs single-item lip purchases, if trackable.
- **Signals**: If attach rate is low, test placement and copy (e.g. headline, CTA wording). If AOV rises but conversion drops, simplify messaging or reduce discount (if any).

---

## 6) Pricing and Discount (optional)

- **No discount**: Bundle at sum of components; value = convenience and clarity.
- **Light discount**: e.g. 10–15% off when buying the set; show "Save $X" or "Save Y%" next to the bundle.
- **Anchoring**: e.g. "X% off when you complete the look" on PDP; avoid overlapping with site-wide sales in a confusing way.
- **Margin**: Ensure bundle margin is acceptable; avoid compressing margin too much on the set.

---

## Implementation checklist (WooCommerce)

1. Create "Complete Lip Look" as a bundle/composite product (or equivalent) with lip liner + lipstick + lip gloss.
2. Add bundle block/section to lip product PDPs with headline, subhead, bullet list, and primary CTA: "Add complete lip look to cart".
3. Add secondary CTAs: "Add [item] only" for each of liner, lipstick, gloss.
4. Ensure one-click add adds the full set; keep single-item add visible.
5. In cart, display "Part of Complete Lip Look" for bundle line(s).
6. Set up analytics for AOV, bundle attach rate, and PDP conversion; review and iterate on copy and placement.
