# Complete the Look — Full Base Kit Strategy

**Client context:** Beauty store with primer, concealer, powder, and brushes. Considering a "Full Base Kit" bundle with ~10% off when buying the set. Need: bundle structure, PDP copy, and tracking plan.

---

## 1) Summary

- **Current gap:** No one-click "complete the look" offer; customers buy primer, concealer, powder, or brushes individually with no clear path to the full base routine. Cross-sell is likely scattered or absent, leaving AOV and attachment opportunity on the table.

- **Recommended bundle type(s):** **Full Base Kit** — primer + concealer + powder + one brush (e.g. powder brush). One hero bundle; optional **Base Essentials** (primer + concealer + powder only, no brush) for lower commitment if you have a distinct brush collection.

- **Top 3 actions:**
  1. **Define and publish the Full Base Kit** — one product/variant or bundle app entry with the four SKUs, 10% off set price, and clear one-line value ("Everything you need for a flawless, long-lasting base").
  2. **Surface the bundle on PDP** — above the fold: headline, 10% savings line, "Add complete look to cart" CTA, plus visible "Add [item] only" so the bundle is never forced.
  3. **Instrument and baseline** — tag bundle adds in analytics, then track AOV, bundle attach rate, and PDP→cart conversion for 2–4 weeks before iterating.

- **Metrics to watch:** AOV (all orders + bundle vs non-bundle), bundle attach rate (% of orders containing the Full Base Kit), PDP conversion rate where the bundle is shown, and margin check on the 10% discount so the bundle stays profitable.

---

## 2) Bundle Definition

- **Logic:** Group by *step* (base routine: prime → correct/cover → set). One hero bundle; keep 3–4 items so the offer is easy to understand.

- **Core bundle (name + SKUs + one-line value):**
  - **Full Base Kit** — Primer + Concealer + Powder + [one brush, e.g. Powder Brush] — *"Everything you need for a flawless, long-lasting base in one click."*

- **Rules:** Max 3–4 items per bundle; one hero product (primer or concealer) plus complements (concealer, powder, brush). Always allow adding single items only.

- **Optional:** **Base Essentials** — Primer + Concealer + Powder (no brush) for customers who already own a brush or want a smaller set. Can use the same 10% off or a smaller discount to keep messaging simple.

---

## 3) Naming and Copy

- **Bundle name:** **Full Base Kit** (or "Complete the Look — Full Base Kit" if you want the phrase in the name).

- **PDP headline:**  
  **"Complete your look — Full Base Kit"**  
  Subhead: *"Primer + concealer + powder + brush. Everything for a flawless, long-lasting base. Save 10% when you complete the look."*

- **Primary CTA:** **"Add complete look to cart"** or **"Get the set — Save 10%"**  
  **Secondary CTA:** **"Add [product name] only"** (per single item).

- **Discount line:** **"Save 10% when you complete the look"** — show next to the bundle price and in the subhead. Optionally: **"Save $X when you buy the set"** if the dollar amount is more compelling.

- **Copy blocks:**
  - **Bullet list (what’s in the bundle):**  
    - [Primer] — smooth canvas, longer wear  
    - [Concealer] — cover and correct  
    - [Powder] — set and blur  
    - [Brush] — even, streak-free application  
    - *One click. One complete base.*
  - **Social proof (if you have data):** e.g. *"Most customers complete the look with the set."* or *"Best value when you complete the set."*

- **Tone:** Match brand (clean/minimal or playful); avoid jargon; focus on outcome (flawless base, one-click convenience, 10% savings). No health/medical claims.

---

## 4) Placement and One-Click UX on PDP

- **Above the fold:** Lead with "Complete your look — Full Base Kit", the 10% savings line, and the bundle price (with optional per-item breakdown). Single primary button: **"Add complete look to cart"** so one click adds the whole set.

- **Clear exit:** Keep "Add [Primer] only", "Add [Concealer] only", etc. visible so the bundle is never the only path. Bundle is the recommended path; single-item add remains obvious.

- **Cart:** When the bundle is added, show a line such as **"Part of Complete the Look — Full Base Kit"** with optional link to remove from set or edit (if your platform supports it).

- **One-click:** Prefer one action to add the entire bundle (single "Add bundle to cart") plus clear individual add-to-cart for each SKU. No multi-step wizard required for the main bundle.

- **Collection/landing (optional):** A "Complete the Look" collection or PDP section linking to Full Base Kit (and Base Essentials if you launch it) helps discovery and SEO.

---

## 5) Pricing

- **10% off the set:** Set bundle price = (Primer + Concealer + Powder + Brush) × 0.90. Show both "Set price: $X" and "You save 10% (or $Y)" on the PDP.

- **Anchoring:** Display **"10% off when you complete the look"** on the PDP. Avoid stacking with site-wide percentage-off sales in a way that confuses customers (e.g. clarify "Set discount applies to the bundle only" if you run store-wide promos).

- **Margin check:** Before launch, confirm that (Primer + Concealer + Powder + Brush) at 10% off still meets your target margin. If any component is low-margin, consider a smaller discount on that item or a 5–8% bundle discount instead. Flag if the bundle would compress margin too much.

---

## 6) Metrics and Validation

- **Primary**
  - **AOV:** Compare AOV for all orders, and for orders that include the Full Base Kit vs. orders that do not. Expect a lift on bundle orders.
  - **Bundle attach rate:** % of orders (or of sessions that add to cart) that include the Full Base Kit. Track weekly; goal is a stable or growing attach rate once placement and copy are live.

- **Secondary**
  - **Conversion rate:** PDP → cart (or PDP → purchase) for pages where the bundle is shown. If conversion drops after adding the bundle, test simplifying the block or reducing visual weight.
  - **Margin:** Periodically check blended margin on bundle vs. non-bundle orders to ensure the 10% discount does not erode profitability.

- **Signals and iteration**
  - **Low attach rate:** Improve placement (higher on PDP), copy ("Save 10% when you complete the look"), or discount visibility.
  - **AOV up, conversion down:** Simplify the bundle (e.g. 3 items instead of 4) or reduce discount and stress convenience over price.
  - **Refunds/returns:** Compare refund/return rate for bundle vs. single-item orders; if higher for bundle, review product fit or expectations (e.g. clarity of what’s in the set).

- **Reporting:** Use consistent names (e.g. "Full Base Kit") in PDP, cart, and analytics so AOV, attach rate, and conversion are easy to segment and report.

---

## Action List

1. **Define** — Create Full Base Kit (primer + concealer + powder + brush), set 10% off price, write one-line value and PDP copy as above.  
2. **Implement** — Add bundle block above the fold on relevant PDPs with "Add complete look to cart" and "Add [item] only"; show "Save 10% when you complete the look" and bundle price.  
3. **Track** — Tag bundle adds, then measure AOV, bundle attach rate, PDP conversion, and margin for 2–4 weeks; iterate on placement and copy based on signals.
